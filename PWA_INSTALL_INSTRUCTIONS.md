# PWA Installation Instructions

## Installing "The Rangels" on iOS Devices

### iPhone/iPad (Safari)

1. **Open Safari** and navigate to your app URL
2. **Tap the Share button** (square with arrow pointing up) at the bottom of the screen
3. **Scroll down** and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. **Edit the name** if desired (default: "The Rangels")
5. **Tap "Add"** in the top right corner

The app icon will now appear on your home screen and will open in full-screen mode without the Safari browser UI.
